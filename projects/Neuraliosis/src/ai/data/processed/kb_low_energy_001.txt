Title: Persistent Low Energy and Fatigue
Category: wellness
Symptoms: extreme tiredness, difficulty with usual activities, mental fog, lack of motivation, persistent after rest
Causes: inadequate sleep, poor nutrition, overtraining, stress, iron deficiency, thyroid issues, illness
Guidance: Fatigue lasting weeks despite adequate rest warrants investigation. Common causes include poor sleep, inadequate nutrition, or overtraining. Ensure you're eating enough calories, especially carbs for training.
Prevention: Get 7-9 hours quality sleep. Eat balanced meals with adequate calories. Include rest days. Manage stress. Consider iron and B12 levels if concerned.
When to see doctor: Fatigue lasting more than 2 weeks despite good sleep and nutrition, fatigue with weight loss or other symptoms, or suspected nutritional deficiency
