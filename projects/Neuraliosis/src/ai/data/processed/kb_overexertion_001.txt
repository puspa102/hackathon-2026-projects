Title: Exercise Overexertion
Category: fitness
Symptoms: extreme fatigue, muscle soreness, decreased performance, elevated heart rate, difficulty sleeping
Causes: too much too soon, inadequate recovery time, ignoring body signals, sudden increase in intensity
Guidance: Overexertion occurs when training volume or intensity increases too rapidly without adequate recovery. Rest days are essential for muscle repair and adaptation. Follow the 10% rule: increase training volume by no more than 10% per week.
Prevention: Have 1-2 complete rest days weekly. Include active recovery (light walking, stretching). Gradually increase workout intensity. Listen to your body.
When to see doctor: Persistent severe fatigue lasting weeks, unexplained muscle pain with weakness, or signs of injury
