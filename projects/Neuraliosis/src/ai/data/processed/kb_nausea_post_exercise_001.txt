Title: Nausea During or After Exercise
Category: fitness
Symptoms: feeling sick to stomach, queasiness, dizziness with nausea, loss of appetite
Causes: eating too close to exercise, dehydration, exercising too intensely too soon, heat stress, anxiety
Guidance: Post-exercise nausea is common and usually not serious. Avoid eating 2-3 hours before intense exercise. Eat light snacks if needed. Start with moderate intensity if new to exercise.
Prevention: Wait 2-3 hours after large meals before intense exercise. Hydrate properly. Build intensity gradually. Exercise in cooler environments.
When to see doctor: Nausea with vomiting and fever, persistent nausea lasting hours, nausea with chest pain or severe dizziness
