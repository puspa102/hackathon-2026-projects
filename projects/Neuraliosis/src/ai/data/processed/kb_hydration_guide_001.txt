Title: Proper Hydration Guidelines
Category: wellness
Symptoms: 
Causes: 
Guidance: Proper hydration is essential for exercise performance and recovery. Drink when thirsty and monitor urine color (pale yellow is ideal). Needs increase with heat, humidity, and exercise intensity. Electrolytes (sodium, potassium) are important for longer workouts.
Prevention: Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily. Before exercise: 16-20 oz 2-3 hours prior. During exercise: 7-10 oz every 10-20 minutes. After: 16-24 oz per pound lost.
When to see doctor: Signs of severe dehydration: extreme thirst, confusion, lack of sweating despite heat, severe dizziness
