Title: Chest Pain During or After Exercise - CRITICAL
Category: cardiovascular_critical
Symptoms: chest pain or pressure, pain radiating to arm or jaw, shortness of breath, nausea, cold sweat
Causes: muscle strain (benign), heart-related issues (serious), anxiety, acid reflux
Guidance: ANY chest pain during or after exercise requires medical evaluation. While muscle strain from exercise is often benign, chest pain can indicate serious cardiovascular issues. Do not ignore or self-diagnose.
Prevention: Get cardiovascular fitness assessment before starting new exercise program, especially if you have risk factors. Warm up gradually.
When to see doctor: ANY chest pain during exercise = STOP EXERCISE IMMEDIATELY AND SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION. This is a medical emergency.
