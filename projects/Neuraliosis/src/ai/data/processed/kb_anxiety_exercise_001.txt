Title: Exercise Anxiety and Panic Attacks
Category: mental_wellness
Symptoms: rapid heartbeat, rapid breathing, chest tightness, dizziness, fear of heart problems, feeling out of control
Causes: health anxiety, past negative experience, fear of overexertion, performance pressure
Guidance: Anxiety during exercise can mimic cardiac symptoms but is usually not dangerous. Recognizing anxiety symptoms helps reduce fear. Gradual exposure and relaxation techniques help. Exercise actually reduces long-term anxiety.
Prevention: Start slowly with exercise. Practice deep breathing. Avoid caffeine before workouts. Progressive training builds confidence.
When to see doctor: Anxiety that interferes with daily life, panic attacks unrelated to exercise, or difficulty distinguishing anxiety from real physical symptoms
