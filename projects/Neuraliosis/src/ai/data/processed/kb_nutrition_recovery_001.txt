Title: Post-Exercise Nutrition and Recovery
Category: wellness
Symptoms: 
Causes: 
Guidance: Recovery nutrition within 30-60 minutes post-exercise accelerates muscle repair. Include carbohydrates to replenish glycogen and protein for muscle repair. Ratio of 3:1 or 4:1 carbs to protein is ideal.
Prevention: Prepare post-workout snacks: banana with peanut butter, Greek yogurt with berries, chocolate milk, or protein smoothie. Include hydration with electrolytes.
When to see doctor: Persistent poor recovery despite good nutrition and sleep warrants medical evaluation
