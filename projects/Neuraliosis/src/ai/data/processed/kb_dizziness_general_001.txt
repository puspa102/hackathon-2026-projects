Title: Dizziness: Causes and When to Worry
Category: general
Symptoms: spinning sensation, lightheadedness, unsteadiness, vertigo, difficulty focusing
Causes: dehydration, low blood pressure, anemia, inner ear issues, anxiety, overexertion, blood sugar changes
Guidance: Dizziness has multiple causes and context matters. Mild dizziness immediately after intense exercise often relates to dehydration or sudden position changes. Persistent dizziness or dizziness with other symptoms may indicate underlying conditions.
Prevention: Stay hydrated. Rise slowly from lying/sitting positions. Eat regular balanced meals. Manage stress. Get adequate sleep.
When to see doctor: Dizziness with chest pain, severe headache, difficulty speaking, weakness, numbness, vision changes, or lasting more than a few days. If dizziness comes suddenly or worsens, seek immediate medical attention.
