Title: Heart Palpitations and Irregular Heartbeat
Category: cardiovascular
Symptoms: rapid heart rate, fluttering sensation, pounding heart, chest discomfort, shortness of breath
Causes: caffeine consumption, stress, dehydration, electrolyte imbalance, overexertion, atrial fibrillation
Guidance: Occasional heart palpitations can result from caffeine, dehydration, or anxiety. Mild palpitations during or after intense exercise are often normal as heart works harder. However, persistent palpitations warrant evaluation.
Prevention: Reduce caffeine intake. Stay hydrated. Manage stress through meditation and breathing exercises. Warm up before intense exercise.
When to see doctor: Frequent palpitations, palpitations with chest pain or shortness of breath, fainting, or persistent irregular heartbeat. This is a critical symptom requiring immediate medical attention.
