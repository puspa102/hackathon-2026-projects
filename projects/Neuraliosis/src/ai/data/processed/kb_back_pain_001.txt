Title: Back Pain and Exercise
Category: musculoskeletal
Symptoms: lower or upper back pain, stiffness, pain radiating to legs, muscle tightness
Causes: poor posture, weak core, tight hamstrings, incorrect form, sudden movements
Guidance: Back pain is common but often preventable with proper form, core strength, and flexibility. Strengthening core muscles supports spine. Stretching hip flexors and hamstrings reduces strain.
Prevention: Maintain proper posture. Do core strengthening exercises 3x weekly. Include flexibility work. Lift with legs, not back. Take breaks if desk job.
When to see doctor: Severe back pain, pain radiating to legs with numbness/tingling, back pain from injury, or pain lasting more than 2 weeks
