Title: Sleep Issues and Recovery
Category: wellness
Symptoms: difficulty falling asleep, waking frequently, not feeling rested, daytime fatigue, mood changes
Causes: excessive late-night exercise, stress, irregular sleep schedule, screen time before bed, caffeine
Guidance: Quality sleep is essential for fitness recovery and immune function. Aim for 7-9 hours nightly. Avoid intense exercise 2-3 hours before bedtime as it raises body temperature and heart rate. Create a cool, dark sleeping environment.
Prevention: Maintain consistent sleep schedule. Exercise earlier in day. Avoid caffeine after 2 PM. Limit screens 30 minutes before bed. Practice relaxation techniques.
When to see doctor: Chronic insomnia lasting weeks, sleep apnea symptoms (loud snoring, gasping during sleep), or extreme daytime sleepiness
