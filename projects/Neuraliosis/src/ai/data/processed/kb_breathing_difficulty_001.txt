Title: Breathing Difficulty - CRITICAL
Category: respiratory_critical
Symptoms: shortness of breath at rest, wheezing, difficulty catching breath, chest tightness, rapid shallow breathing
Causes: asthma, exercise-induced bronchoconstriction, severe deconditioning, allergies, heart issues, anxiety
Guidance: While some breathlessness during intense exercise is normal, any persistent difficulty breathing warrants medical evaluation. Exercise-induced asthma is common but manageable with proper treatment.
Prevention: Get fitness assessment before starting program. Warm up before intense exercise. Use prescribed inhalers if needed. Gradually increase intensity.
When to see doctor: Difficulty breathing that persists after exercise stops, wheezing, repeated episodes, or breathing difficulty at rest. Seek immediate emergency care if severe.
