Title: Low Blood Sugar During/After Exercise
Category: fitness
Symptoms: dizziness, shakiness, difficulty concentrating, irritability, excessive sweating, weakness
Causes: exercising fasted, skipping meals, prolonged intense exercise, inadequate carbohydrate intake
Guidance: Low blood sugar (hypoglycemia) during exercise can cause dizziness and weakness. Eat a small carbohydrate-rich snack 30-60 minutes before exercise. For exercise longer than 60 minutes, consume 30-60g carbs per hour during activity. Include protein and fat to sustain energy.
Prevention: Eat balanced meals with carbs, protein, and healthy fats. Never exercise on completely empty stomach. Have quick carbs available (banana, energy bar, sports drink).
When to see doctor: Frequent hypoglycemic episodes, inability to recover with carbs, or suspected diabetes symptoms
